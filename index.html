Internal Mechanics

The Quantum Engine: Gates & Circuits

At the core of QUBIT_SIM lies a mathematical transformation matrix engine capable of processing universal gate sets with zero floating-point drift.

H

Hadamard (H)

The fundamental operation for creating superposition. Maps the basis state |0⟩ to (|0⟩ + |1⟩)/√2, placing the qubit in a state of equal probability.

X

Pauli-X (NOT)

The quantum equivalent of the classical NOT gate. Rotates the state vector 180 degrees around the X-axis of the Bloch sphere.

CN

Controlled-NOT (CNOT)

A 2-qubit gate essential for entanglement. Flips the target qubit if and only if the control qubit is in state |1⟩.

CCX

Toffoli (CCNOT)

A 3-qubit universal gate for classical reversible computation. It provides the basis for constructing any Boolean function within a quantum circuit.
